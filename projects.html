Global Electronics Retailer

This is a multi-page Power BI dashboard analysing the sales performance, product performance, store performance, and delivery performance. The data was first pre-processed using Python where RFM analysis was conducted to segment the customers and products based on their performance. This project reveals the major painpoint of the business which is its poor delivery time for customers online orders. Regions, products and brands causing much of this delay where identified for the business to take decisive action on solving the problem.

Health Analytics of Patients

This one-page interactive dashboard explores patients' hospital utilisation patterns and insurance coverage within Massachusetts General Hospital using synthetic data from 2011 to 2022. Developed in Power BI, the project intergrates clinical and financial dimensions to illuminate how patients engage with hospital services and how insurance coverage impacts access and cost.
