Global Electronics Retailer Dashboard

Project Description

Tools: Python, Excel, DAX, and Power BI

This Power BI dashboard provides a variety of analyses that resolves the painpoints of Global Electronics Retailer. It is an all round dashboard that offers insights into the various units of Global Electronics Retailer. It offers the various units of the business the ability to generate insights individually using different and relevant KPIs as well as filters important for each unit of the business.

Project Objectives

The objectives of this project are simplified and stated as follows:

  1. To develop a unit-specific analytics dashboard to support decision-making across different units,
  2. Identifying top-performing products by revenue and profit,
  3. Analysing revenue and profit growth trends,
  4. Evaluating market performance by region,
  5. Profiling high-impact product and customer segments, and
  6. Highlighting operational bottlenecks, delivery, and logistics issues

Discovery and Presentation of Insights

  1. Developed a 5-page dashboard consisting of Executive Summary, Product Performance, Customer Analysis, Sales Channel Performance, and Delivery & Logistics Analysis.
  2. Filtering by year, in 2018, Adventure Works Desktop PC233 led in both revenue and profit.
  3. Revenue, units sold, and profits peaked in 2018, followed by a decline likely impacted by the COVID-19.
  4. North America (U.S. & Canada) emerged as the strongest market for Global Electronics Retailer.
  5. Champion customers and Best Seller products contribute the most to business performance, therefore, these segments require priority attention and retention strategies.
  6. Despite fluctuations in average order value, in-store sales consistently outperformed online sales in revenue, often by more than 3x.
  7. Online sales channel has critical delivery issues. Only 7.23% of orders were delivered on time in 2016, which is a major problem for both online marketing and delivery/logistics units.
  8. Also, critical delays (>90 days) cluster in Q1 each year, showing the need to pay attention to this period.
  9. The Contoso brand and Home Appliances product category recorded the highest delivery delays as indicated by the delivery severity index (DSI), a metric that quantifies the severity and frequency of delivery delays.

Conclusion

This project demonstrates how thoughtful data engineering and visual storytelling can drive strategic decisions in product management, customer segmentation, channel optimisation, and logistics performance.
